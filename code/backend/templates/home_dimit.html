Our Story

An online tool for estimating fish age from otolith images using Deep Learning.

 

For more details how the AI platform works, please see:

DeepOtolith: A web-tool for fish age estimation from otolith images using deep learning (Documentation).

Motive

Accurate estimation of fish ageing is crucial for assessing the status of fish stocks and developing management plans for ensuring their sustainability. The prevailing methods of fish ageing are based on manual readings of otolith images by experts, a process that is often time-consuming and costly.

Future Work

Our long term aim is to extend this tool by adding more fish species and improving the predictability of existing ones.

Contributing

DeepOtolith welcomes collaborations with researchers who want to contribute towards the automation of fish ageing.
